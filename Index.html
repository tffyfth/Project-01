About Me

I am Tiffany Faith Dalaya, a second-year student at National Teacher College studying BSIT. Besides being burned out from studying, I sometimes engage in something to relieve stress and have fun.
K-pop music is something I really enjoy listening to. I like to watch
K-pop idols sing and dance. K-pop, or Korean pop music, is a well-known genre of music that originated in South Korea. In K-pop, there are many delightful songs, dances, fashions, beauty trends, and other social media trends. K-pop artists make my day complete because they make me smile and laugh.

I also enjoy watching Korean drama, or Kdrama, whenever I have a lot of free time or it is my day of relaxation. I can complete every episode in a single day. Every Korean drama that is produced has plots, genres, casts, and cinematography that I absolutely enjoy. They have a clever and original strategy for getting viewers to enjoy and watch the drama.

I also read stories from Wattpad and Jonaxx Stories online when I'm under pressure at school. I enjoy reading romance novels, especially fantasy and slow-burning love romances. Reading my favorite stories makes me feel better since the characters are interesting and I can relate to them. When I feel like I can only rely on myself, this helps me have a source of bonding, and reading stories can boost my energy and mood. In this Website I will share to you these things that I like whenever I feel stressed. I have some of my Favorite K-pop and K-dramas and also I have recommended stories that you can read online.

Books

Heartless

This book for me is wonderful. It's just that it could be a little problematic for others. Jonaxx stories needs a very critical thinking because the idea that you grasp will only be stuck at bad or good nothing in between. Same with this book, if you have a weak mindset and easy to falter stands you'll take wrong idea.

Charm Academy

First book ever that I've read on Wattpad and I'm grateful that this particular story was first one I had read cause it created such memorable reading moments for me. Plot was fun and great!

Every Beast Needs A Beauty

At first, I was really hesitating to read this on Wattpad. But I took those words back when I read the 7 chapters spontaneously without stopping. I had a hard time understanding other vocabularies, but I still survived! Just like her other stories, every chapter is full of scenes that will keep you reading and reading without getting bored. I recommend you to read this 10/10. If you love cliché with a little bit of spice, you have to read this.

Give In To You

I got more feels in this story. I felt the characters emotions more as well. All the emotions, sensations that this story made me feel—I’ll never forget them. The way it made the butterflies in my stomach burst, the way it made me feel annoyed, frustrated, & the ultra mega super duper kilig that I felt—it’ll forever stay in my system. This story made me cuss countless of times because there are points where all I could do is to curse those certain characters and wish they were dead.

Olympus Academy

A great way to read a whole series that was fun and entertaining. Though I am not always sure who is who sometimes. Add in the mystery, suspense, and trying to survive and wow what a series. A great blend of mythology, magic, quests, enemies, creatures and teenage angst and you get a great academy series.

Training To Love

his is absolutely one of my favorite stories in Wattpad. Well, it is actually recommended to read this book first before trying the latter. But no worries, for the latter is still understandable even if you haven’t read this book yet. It was a well written story. I don’t even think there’s something I can complain of. This is absolutely not your typical cliché story. The story line was great and the plot is beautifully executed. There are of course a lot of extra sweet lines in the story but I salute the author for not letting it sounds too cheesy or too corny.

My Favorite K-pop/K-Drama

BTS

BTS are multi-talented persons and passionate with their music and performace. They write and produce their own songs and started at their lowest because of that they gain popularity and became successful.

Seventeen

Seventeen is a group consist of 13 members, 3 Groups (Rap Line, Vocal Line, and Performance Line) and they are 1 team that makes them Seventeen. They are considere as "self-producing" idol group because the members are involved with songwriting and cheorography. Seventeen are savage, funny, and super lively group that will make your day happy.

TXT

TXT or Tomorrow by Together consist of 5 members. All of them are great with their vocals and dances that's why they are satisfying to listen and watched their performance on stage. They are all good-looking men and fun to watched because of their funny personalities.

Twice

Twice have a cheerful and colorful concept of their songs and dance moves. Each of their albums has a distinct features of their chorus and dance moves. All of the girls has their own charisma when performing that you will end up liking them.

Blackpink

Blackpink is the most popular girl group because of their undeniable stage presence and their music. Their music is such a masterpiece because they talk about independence and vulnerability and their dance become popular in the internet. They are known as fashion icons in the K-pop industry because of their outfits in their every performance.

Enhypen

Enhypen is what excites the fans right now. They are consist of 7 members and they are considered as the next BTS to achieve what BTS succeed. Their group is hardworking, creative, and great performers. Among all the 4th generation k-poop group they have unique concept with beautiful vocals and drop dead handsomeness.

Le Sserafim

Le Sserafim is one of the rookie group that gain popularity with their debut songs. They are multi-talented girls and they composed their music. Their concept leans towards the "girl crush" image and each members shows their strength and elegance.

Newjeans

Newjeans is the newest K-pop group that debuted with their new song 'Hype Boy' and 'Attention'. They easily gain popularity because each members has a pretty good vocals and it is interesting to hear them singing. Their songs are great and it feels sleek, cool, clean, and groovy.

The Glory

This K-drama has a well-written storyline. The Glory showed the brutal reality of bullying that can deeply affect a person's life. This raises awareness to the viewers that can teach us some great values that we must live by. It also has a star-studded cast because the casts creates the best acting and balance in the story.

All Of Us Are Dead

This is one of the best zombie apocalypse horror genre show. The casts emotions is at their peak and they are supporting their acting as a group. This is a nice stress reliever that you gonna get on and will enjoy watching.

Alchemy Of Souls

Alchemy of Souls has intersting chain of events. This is a fantasy drama with the touch of romantic comedy that brings exciting events and theories to the viewers.

Extraordinary Attorney Woo

This is a great show, each episodes has a different cases and great solution led by Attorney Woo who has autism spectrum disorder. It is insparational and will give you a lesson.
